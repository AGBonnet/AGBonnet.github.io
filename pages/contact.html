The Memory Palace is an ongoing project linking art, poetry, symbolism and the people in my life into one story.

22 Tarot figures from the Major Arcana are each associated with someone who left their indelible mark on me. All are embodied through archetypal forms in a seven part poem.

Each archetype will be the subject of one artwork in any medium.

As of now, a single artwork is finished: Death of Narcissus
