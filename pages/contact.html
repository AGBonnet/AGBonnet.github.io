The Memory Palace is an ongoing project linking art, poetry, symbolism and the people in my life into one story.

Tarot figures from the Major Arcana are each associated with someone who left their mark on me. Each archetype is embodied within a seven part poem and will be the subject of an artwork in any medium.

As of now, only one artwork is finished: Death of Narcissus.

Visual Flow is a cloud of images flashing directly on the nervous system, a representation of how other artists' work consciously or subconsciously informs my own.
